Recipe for Schnitzel

German Style Schnitzel

This version of schnitzel should only take 5 minutes to prepare. It's eays and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 5 chicken breasts, flattened.
  • 1 egg
  • Panko crumbs

Steps:

  1. Coat the chicken in the eggs.
  2. Cover the chicken in the panko crumbs, and redip in the eggs.
  3. Once in the eggs, redip back into the panko crumbs (to double coat it).
  4. Fry it in a pan until chicken is cooked.
