Recipe for Goulash

Hungarian Style Goulash

This version of Goulash is Hungarian-style. It is made to simmer for hours It's delicious with bread, or on its own. It tastes like a thick, warm hug.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Potatoes
  • 4 Carrots
  • 5 Mushrooms
  • 1 Cup beef stock
  • 1 lb chuck stew beef
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • salt & pepper
  • 16 oz can diced tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Coat the meat with the flour.
  2. Sear the meat.
  3. Add the sliced veggies to the pot.
  4. Fill the pot with water and stock until barely covering the veggies.
  5. Simmer for 4 hours.
  6. Serve with bread or on its own.
